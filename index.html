15-Week Journey

February 3 — May 14, 2026

Weeks 1-4 • Feb 3 - Mar 2

Foundation

Learn form, build the habit, establish baseline

  • Master compound lift technique
  • Establish gym + MMA routine
  • Dial in 3,000+ calorie intake
  • Week 4: Deload (light weights)
  • Target: +3-4 lbs gained
Weeks 5-8 • Mar 3 - Mar 30

Building

Increase weight steadily, add MMA intensity

  • Progressive overload in full effect
  • Start light sparring at Main Street
  • Aim for PRs on all compounds
  • Week 8: Deload
  • Target: +6-8 lbs total
Weeks 9-12 • Mar 31 - Apr 27

Intensification

Push limits, increase MMA volume

  • Heavier weights, lower reps on compounds
  • Regular sparring sessions
  • Consider 2nd MMA class on Thu
  • Week 12: Deload
  • Target: +12-15 lbs total
Weeks 13-15 • Apr 28 - May 14

Peak

Test maxes, maintain gains through finals

  • Test 1-rep maxes on big lifts
  • Maintain training through finals
  • Reduce volume if needed for exams
  • Celebrate transformation
  • Target: 165-170 lbs

Main Street Classes

Your MMA training at Main Street Boxing & Muay Thai

Muay Thai All Levels

Mon/Tue/Wed/Thu • 6:30-7:30 PM

Core striking development. Learn kicks, knees, elbows, and clinch work. Your bread and butter class.

Muay Thai Pads

Tue/Thu • 7:30-8:30 PM

Technical work on pads with partners. Great for timing and combinations. Stack this after All Levels on Thursday.

MMA All Levels

Mon/Wed/Fri • 5:00-6:00 PM

Learn to combine striking and grappling. Perfect for building a complete fight game.

Amateur Boxing

Mon/Tue/Wed • 7:30-8:30 PM

Hand speed, footwork, head movement. Excellent complement to Muay Thai. Hit this on Fridays after class.

Muay Thai Sparring

Saturday • 11:00 AM-12:00 PM

Apply everything you learned under pressure. Your weekly skill test. Don't skip this.

Fight Fit

Daily • Various times

High-intensity conditioning. Good cardio option if you need extra burn, but not required with your lifting schedule.
